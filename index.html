About the willa

Welcome to Villa Esmeralda – comfortable living in Finestrat, Costa Blanca! With its modern furnishing and fittings, this villa offers a truly blissful experience. Villa Esmeralda offers six double bedrooms, two with their own en-suite shower room, and two shared bathrooms.

The villa forms part of the residential complex Camporrosso Village, hence, you will have access to communal facilities which include large open swimming pool with shallow-water area for kids, heated indoor swimming pool, gym, sauna, paddle tennis court, and kids play area. Built over 2 floors, this magnificent property has 6 double bedrooms and 4 bathrooms.

Entering the villa you find an open plan living-dining area, and kitchen with island that leads to the terrace with infinity pool with beautiful views over Benidorm skyline. On the same floor, there are two bedrooms with double beds and en-suite bathrooms, two doble bedrooms with individual beds, and one shared bathroom with shower.

On the ground floor you will find two double bedrooms, one bathroom with bathtub, and large leisure area with pool table, football table, tennis table, and outdoor basketball hoop. The outdoor space is equipped with sun loungers and a gas barbecue so you can enjoy your time with friends. The villa is equipped with internet, alarm system, ducted air conditioning on the first floor, and splits on the ground floor. On-site parking. You can be sure that all guests will experience maximum comfort! .
